KALACHITRA

Sketching is the most efficient way of expressing yourself

Kalachitra is the most highly anticipated Art event of Saptrang’20. Sketching is the most efficient way of expressing yourself; it is a medium that transcends words and encourages individuals to visualize and reinvent their creative ideas. Kalachitra aims to bring together the prevailing generation's most artistic minds and provide them with a platform to compete. The premise is simple: to represent a certain given topic in your own style and utilize different skills.

  • Participation: Solo
  • Duration: 1.5 hrs
  • Prizes Worth: 8,000
  • DOWNLOAD

FUSION ART

Tattoo and Mehendi

Mehendi is a form of body art since ancient times in India. While on the other hand, tattoos is a form of body art which was initially popular in the west. The two bring a visual treat to the eyes. This competition invites all the artistic individuals from all around to come in the limelight by winning this face-off. Get ready for the battle.

  • Participation: Duet (one painting, one to be painted on)
  • Duration: 1.5 hrs
  • Prizes Worth: 7,000
  • DOWNLOAD

BEST OUTTA WASTE

"One man's waste is another man's treasure".

And the one who creates something unique out of this waste is the real winner here!Saptrang’20 presents to you yet another exciting art event, that will have you bringing out all your artistic potential to the table. Premise: To create a unique model out of the seemingly meaningless, everyday waste items provided, based on the theme given on the spot. The more imaginative and inventive, the more points you rack up! Bring your friends in groups of 2-3 and show us what the youth is capable of creating!

  • Participation: Group of 2 or 3
  • Duration: 1.5 hrs
  • Prizes Worth: 8,000
  • DOWNLOAD

SHOW OF HANDS

“Pop your poppers and clink your glasses.”

Unparalleled freedom of self-expression in which one needs to go all out and put all their feelings on paper by dipping their fingers in paint.Saptrang’20 presenting an exciting event which will help you make a lasting colourful memory.Premise- to paint, not by brush but using your fingers, to create something fresh which will highly engage the audience's mind towards your canvas.

  • Participation: Solo
  • Duration: 1.5 hrs
  • Prizes Worth: 8,000
  • DOWNLOAD

FACE PAINTING

The integration of paintings on an out of the ordinary canvas has given rise to another level of unique art called face painting. With your canvas as a face, and theme given on the spot Saptrang’20 brings to you this unique event that will have participants turn to an art style not often explored.

  • Participation: Duet (one painting, one to be painted on)
  • Duration: 1.5 hrs
  • Prizes Worth: 8,000
