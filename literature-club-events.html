PARLIAMENTARY DEBATE

“Without debate, without criticism, no administration and no country can succeed and no republic can survive.”

Parliamentary Debate is modelled based on the British Parliamentary System. It’s more oratorical, witty, and accessible to general audiences, featuring lesser jargons and fewer theoretical arguments. As members of the House, each team will stand for either Proposition or Opposition. In the same way as the Parliament, the Proposition supports the motion while the Opposition opposes it. Here we call upon the masters and the amateurs in the field of parliamentary debate to come over in a team of 3-4 members and debate until you resolve!
